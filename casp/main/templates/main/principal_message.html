“

Education is not merely about acquiring knowledge — it is about igniting a passion for learning that lasts a lifetime.

Dear Students, Parents, and Well-wishers,

It is with great pride and a deep sense of responsibility that I welcome you to the College of Applied Science, Puthuppally. Our institution has stood as a pillar of academic excellence for decades, nurturing generations of bright minds who have gone on to make a meaningful difference in society and the world at large.

At CAS Puthuppally, we believe that a college is more than just a place of learning — it is a community where students discover their identities, build lifelong friendships, and develop the skills and values that will guide them throughout their lives. Our dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and vibrant campus life all work together to create an environment where every student can thrive.

We are committed to providing our students with the best possible education, combining rigorous academic training with practical exposure to the real world. Our curriculum is designed to be relevant, forward-looking, and responsive to the evolving needs of industry and society. We emphasize critical thinking, creativity, ethical leadership, and a spirit of inquiry — qualities that are essential for success in the 21st century.

I invite all prospective students to be a part of this dynamic and inspiring community. Come, be a part of our journey towards excellence. Together, we will build a future that is bright, meaningful, and full of promise.

“The roots of education are bitter, but the fruit is sweet.” — Aristotle

Prof. Binu Suguthan
Principal — College of Applied Science, Puthuppally
