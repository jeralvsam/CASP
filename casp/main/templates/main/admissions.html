Admissions

Admission to all courses is based on the rules and regulations formulated by the IHRD and Mahatma Gandhi University. We follow a transparent merit-based selection process. Of the total seats, 50% are merit seats filled by the University through Centralized Allotment Process (CAP) and 50% are filled by the College Management.

1

Apply Online

Visit the university CAP portal or the college website to fill out the application form. Ensure all details are accurate.

2

Merit List

Candidates will be shortlisted based on their academic performance in the qualifying examination (Plus Two/Degree).

3

Interview

Shortlisted candidates must appear for an interview with original documents for verification.

4

Admission

Pay the semester fees and submit the necessary certificates to confirm your admission.

Eligibility Criteria

Program Stream Minimum Qualification
Undergraduate B.Sc Computer Science Pass in Plus Two with Mathematics as a subject.
Undergraduate B.Sc Electronics Pass in Plus Two with Physics/Maths/Chemistry.
Undergraduate B.Com / BBA Pass in Plus Two (Any stream) with minimum 45% marks.
Postgraduate M.Sc Computer Science B.Sc CS / BCA with minimum 50% aggregate.
Postgraduate MCA Any Degree with Maths at +2 or Degree level.
