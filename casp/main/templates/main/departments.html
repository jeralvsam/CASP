Computer Science

Established in 1993, the Department of Computer Science is the flagship department of our institution. We are committed to imparting high-quality technical education, fostering innovation, and preparing students for the dynamic IT industry. Our curriculum combines theoretical foundations with practical application, supported by state-of-the-art computer laboratories.
