Who We Are

Introduction & Overview

College of Applied Science, Puthuppally, established in 1993, is managed by the Institute of Human Resources Development (IHRD), a Government of Kerala undertaking. We are committed to providing quality education in science, technology, and commerce.

Located in the serene village of Puthuppally, our campus provides an ideal environment for academic pursuits. Over the decades, we have evolved into a premier institution offering undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Computer Science, Electronics, and Commerce.

Our Journey

History & Milestones

1
1993

Inception

The college was established under the management of the Institute of Human Resources Development (IHRD), Kottayam.

2
1995

First Batches

Began undergraduate degree programs in Computer Science and Electronics, affiliated with MG University.

3
2002

Postgraduate Launch

Introduced M.Sc Computer Science and M.Sc Electronics to promote advanced research and core competencies.

4
2010

Commerce Stream

Added the Department of Commerce and Management to address the growing demand in business and financial education.

5
2023

30 Years Celebrations

Celebrated 30 years of academic distinction, research excellence, and outstanding career placements.
