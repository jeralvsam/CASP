Who We Are

Introduction & Overview

College of Applied Science, Puthuppally, established in 1993, is managed by the Institute of Human Resources Development (IHRD), a Government of Kerala undertaking. We are committed to providing quality education in science, technology, and commerce.

Located in the serene village of Puthuppally, our campus provides an ideal environment for academic pursuits. Over the decades, we have evolved into a premier institution offering undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Computer Science, Electronics, and Commerce.
