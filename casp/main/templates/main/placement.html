Placement Overview

The Training and Placement Cell of CAS Puthuppally plays a vital role in locating job opportunities for Undergraduates and Postgraduates passing out from the college. The cell operates year-round to facilitate contacts between companies and graduates. We ensure that our students are industry-ready by providing them with necessary training in aptitude, soft skills, and technical knowledge.

Our rigorous placement process includes pre-placement talks, written tests, group discussions, and personal interviews. We have a consistent track record of placing our students in top-tier IT and Core companies.

Training Activities

  • Aptitude Training: Regular sessions to improve mathematical and logical reasoning skills.
  • Soft Skills Development: Workshops on communication, leadership, and team building.
  • Mock Interviews: Simulated interview sessions conducted by alumni and industry experts.
  • Resume Building: Guidance on creating professional resumes and cover letters.
  • Technical Workshops: Hands-on sessions on the latest technologies like Python, AI, and IoT.
