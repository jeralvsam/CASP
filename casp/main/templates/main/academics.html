Curriculum & Excellence

Our institution offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs affiliated with Mahatma Gandhi University. The curriculum is designed to provide students with a strong theoretical foundation combined with practical skills to meet industry demands.

Undergraduate

Bachelor's Degree Programs (3 Years)

B.Sc Computer Science

Focuses on programming, algorithms, data structures, and system design. Prepares students for the IT industry.

6 Semesters 40 Seats

B.Sc Electronics

Covers analog and digital circuits, embedded systems, VLSI, and modern communication technologies.

6 Semesters 40 Seats

BCA

Bachelor of Computer Applications. Emphasizes software development, web engineering, and database management.

6 Semesters 40 Seats

B.Com Computer App.

Integrates commerce education with computer applications, accounting software, and business management.

6 Semesters 50 Seats

BBA

Bachelor of Business Administration. Foundational course in management, marketing, HR, and entrepreneurship.

6 Semesters 40 Seats
Postgraduate

Master's Degree Programs (2 Years)

MCA

Master of Computer Applications. Advanced program in software engineering, cloud computing, and application development.

4 Semesters 30 Seats

M.Sc Computer Science

Focuses on Advanced Algorithms, AI, Data Mining, and Machine Learning research methodologies.

4 Semesters 20 Seats

M.Sc Electronics

Specialized training in Signal Processing, Embedded Systems, and Control Systems. Includes project work.

4 Semesters 20 Seats

Academic Resources

State-of-the-art Computer and Electronics laboratories equipped with high-performance systems and testing equipment.

Digital Campus

High-speed Wi-Fi connectivity across campus and access to e-learning platforms like INFLIBNET.
